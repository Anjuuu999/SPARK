Feedback & Program Directory

Explore real ways to get involved across every part of climate action, from saving water to greener transport, planting trees, and cutting energy waste at home. Browse the programmes below, see what others thought, and leave your own feedback.

Programme Directory

Scroll sideways to browse all programmes.

Community Water-Saving Challenge

A 2-week household challenge to cut water use through simple habits - shorter showers, fixing leaks, and reusing greywater for plants.

Colombo Cycle-to-Uni Scheme

Swap one car or bus trip a week for cycling. Weekly group rides help new cyclists learn safe routes around the city.

Vihara Maha Devi Park Tree Drive

Plant 100 native trees at Vihara Maha Devi Park with fellow students and local volunteers. All tools and saplings provided.

Home Energy Swap Workshop

A hands-on session on switching to LED bulbs, unplugging idle devices, and simple insulation fixes to cut home energy waste.

Community Garden Project

Help grow and maintain a shared vegetable and herb garden open to the neighbourhood - a low-effort way to green up unused urban space.
